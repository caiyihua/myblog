# css的vertical-align运用

# 垂直居中

父节点display：table-cell ； vertical-align: middle

<div style="width: 100vw; height: 500px; vertical-align: middle; display: table-cell">
    <div style="width: 100px; height: 100px; background: #3c59c9;"></div>
</div>

# 相对居中

子节点display: inline-block; vertical-align: middle 一定需要2个+元素 做对比才能出效果

<div style="width: 100vw; height: 500px">
    <div style="width: 100px; height: 100px; background: #3c59c9; display: inline-block; vertical-align: middle"></div>
    <div style="width: 100px; height: 200px; background: #3c59c9; display: inline-block; vertical-align: middle"></div>
</div>

# 文字元素向下对齐

要和字对齐的元素vertical-align: text-bottom middle text-top

<div style="width: 100vw; height: 500px">
    <p style=" display: inline-block">213453232435324543245</p>
    <div style="width: 100px; height: 200px; background: #3c59c9; display: inline-block; vertical-align: text-bottom"></div>
</div>
